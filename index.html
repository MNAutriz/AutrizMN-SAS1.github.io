Learnings about SAS as a Computer Science Student

As a computer science student, this class has been surprisingly relevant to my journey. In a field often perceived as purely technical, Self and Society reminded me that technology does not exist in isolation—it’s deeply tied to people, society, and ethics. Understanding myself better helps me approach my studies with greater purpose. I now think more critically about how my skills can be used to solve real-world problems and positively impact society. For example, when I work on projects, I ask myself how the systems I build will affect users and communities. This reflective mindset pushes me to go beyond just coding for functionality and instead design solutions with empathy and social responsibility.

In reflecting on whether I have a stable sense of self or if I’m still in the process of discovering who I am, I realize it’s not a clear-cut answer. My understanding of myself feels like an ongoing journey which is a continuous process of construction and reconstruction. This is something I’ve come to appreciate more through concepts discussed in Self and Society. The class emphasized that the "self" is not just an isolated entity but is shaped by social interactions, cultural norms, and personal experiences. This aligns with the symbolic interactionist perspective, where meaning and identity emerge through relationships and social roles. I now see that my sense of self evolves as I encounter different environments and engage with others. For instance, my roles as a student, a worker, and someone striving for personal goals are all part of how I construct who I am. Each of these roles exposes me to expectations and values that influence my identity. This process of self-construction is not static; it’s fluid. There are times when I feel confident and grounded in who I am, but other moments reveal that I’m still discovering new parts of myself. It’s this balance acknowledging stability while embracing growth that makes my personal journey both challenging and meaningful.

MANIFESTO

Integrating all Information through the Manifesto

My Personalized Manifesto

The essence of self-awareness begins with understanding the perspectives of self—how we see ourselves and how we relate to the world around us. This manifesto serves as my declaration of self-discovery and integration, a roadmap for embracing my identity while navigating the complexities of life. I recognize that my journey is influenced by various factors: my biological makeup, which shapes my instincts and abilities; my personality, which drives my thoughts and behaviors; and my ongoing process of identity development, which helps me define my purpose and values. As I integrate these elements, I strive to achieve balance across all areas of my life. Whether through my passion for computer science or my commitment to personal well-being, I am on a continuous path of learning, evolving, and becoming. This manifesto reflects my desire to align my professional aspirations with personal authenticity, ensuring that I grow holistically while contributing to the greater good.

My Core Beliefs and Values

  • Pursuit of Knowledge and Innovation: I value intellectual curiosity and creativity, constantly seeking to learn and innovate in fields like artificial intelligence. I believe in using my knowledge to contribute to solutions that address societal challenges.
  • Integrity and Ethical Responsibility: Honesty and ethics are cornerstones of my character, whether in personal relationships or professional endeavors. I strive to make decisions that honor my values and respect others.
  • Holistic Balance: I aim to integrate intellectual growth, physical health, and emotional well-being. By doing so, I can live a life of fulfillment and purpose.
  • Resilience and Adaptability: Challenges are opportunities for growth. My ability to adapt and persevere reflects my commitment to personal and professional development.
  • Collaboration and Empathy: I value teamwork and meaningful connections, fostering understanding and creativity. Through collaboration and empathy, I aim to contribute to inclusive and innovative solutions.

My Health Manifesto

Health is a holistic concept that encompasses physical, mental, emotional, and spiritual well-being, shaping how we view ourselves and connect with others. It is not just the absence of illness but a continuous journey of balance and growth. My health manifesto reflects my commitment to nurturing all dimensions of well-being, grounded in self-awareness and acceptance. Central to this is how I perceive myself—embracing my identity, body image, personality, and the biological influences that shape me. Health also reflects the impact of culture and ethnicity, recognizing how traditions and experiences inform my values and aspirations. By honoring these influences, I seek to build a sense of belonging and empathy for others. Sexuality and identity development highlight the importance of authenticity and self-expression, integral to building meaningful relationships. Mental health, a cornerstone of well-being, requires resilience, mindfulness, and seeking help when necessary. Prioritizing emotional regulation and nurturing uplifting relationships are key elements of this journey. This manifesto is a pledge to align my actions and beliefs with a vision of holistic health—balancing ambition with rest, strength with vulnerability, and independence with connection. It is a declaration of growth, grounded in compassion, curiosity, and a commitment to becoming the best version of myself.

  • Physical Health: Engage in sports like volleyball and badminton, maintain nutritious meals, and follow consistent sleep routines. I also I cultivate a positive relationship with my physical self, appreciating my body as a vessel for experiences, growth, and expression by rejecting societal pressures and embracing self-acceptance, I prioritize health, confidence, and inner strength over perfection.
  • Mental and Emotional Well-being: Practice mindfulness, celebrate small wins, and maintain meaningful connections with loved ones. I also I strive to cultivate emotional balance by addressing stress, seeking support when needed, and practicing gratitude and optimism.
  • Intellectual and Professional Growth: Dedicate daily time to learning, set achievable goals, and reflect on the societal impact of my work. This also allows me to seek to honor my individuality while recognizing the interconnectedness of my identity with the people and cultures around me.
  • Spiritual and Creative Fulfillment: Embrace gratitude and balance technical pursuits with creative outlets for inspiration and joy by embracing both consistency and adaptability, I strive to stay authentic while evolving with life’s challenges and opportunities.

Commitment to Innovation and Growth

This manifesto is my compass for navigating life as a computer science enthusiast, a learner, and a person striving for holistic well-being. It integrates my passions for technology, my love for learning, and my desire to maintain balance in all areas of life. I will revisit these principles regularly, evolving them as I continue to grow, innovate, and contribute to the world.
